About Me

My name is Keziah Bless Lacap, an aspiring Information Technology student and the Chief Layout Artist at The Technology Advocate, the official student publication of our campus, where I lead the visual design and layout direction to ensure each issue is engaging, cohesive, and impactful. In addition, I serve as a Media Associate, contributing to content creation, digital media management, and promotional initiatives, which has helped me develop both creative and organizational skills while collaborating with writers, editors, and fellow artists.

Passionate about design and technology, I enjoy exploring new software, experimenting with typography, color theory, and composition, and creating visuals that balance aesthetics with functionality.

Through these roles, I have honed my ability to deliver innovative, purposeful designs, mentor peers, and thrive in fast-paced creative environments, all while embracing opportunities to grow as a designer, communicator, and leader.
